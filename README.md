# 🎬 Amazon Prime Video Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

The Amazon Prime Video Dashboard is an interactive Power BI project designed to analyze and visualize Amazon Prime Video's content library. The dashboard provides insights into content distribution, genres, ratings, release trends, and global availability, helping users better understand the platform's catalog.

---

## 🎯 Objective

The goal of this dashboard is to explore Amazon Prime Video's content and answer key questions such as:

* What type of content dominates the platform?
* Which genres are the most popular?
* How has content production changed over the years?
* Which ratings are most common?
* How is content distributed across different countries?

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* **Power BI Desktop** – Dashboard development
* **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation
* **DAX** – KPI calculations and measures
* **Data Modeling** – Building relationships and analytics

---

## 📊 Dashboard Highlights

### Key KPIs

* Total Titles: **9,655**
* Total Ratings: **25**
* Total Genres: **519**
* Total Directors: **5,771**
* Content Period: **1920 – 2021**

### Visualizations Included

* 🌍 Country-wise content distribution map
* 📈 Content growth by release year
* 🎭 Genre analysis
* ⭐ Ratings distribution
* 🎬 Movies vs TV Shows comparison
* 📊 Interactive KPI cards

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* Movies account for **80.82%** of all titles, while TV Shows represent **19.18%**.
* Drama is the most popular genre on the platform.
* Content growth accelerated significantly after 2010.
* Amazon Prime Video hosts content from countries around the world.
* Teen and family-oriented content forms a large portion of the catalog.

---

## 💡 Business Value

This dashboard helps content analysts, media researchers, and business stakeholders understand content trends, audience targeting, and platform growth. It enables data-driven decisions regarding content strategy and market analysis.
